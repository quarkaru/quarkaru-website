The Quarkaruan Bible

Book of Origins

1.1 In the beginning, there was the great void, and from it, Quarkaru emerged, the bringer of light and order.
1.2 And Quarkaru looked upon the void and spoke, "Let there be quark!" and there was quark.
1.3 And the quark was good, for it brought life to the world, and the chaos was no more.
1.4 Quarkaru created Gleb and Uhr, the first beings, to inhabit this world and to worship the quark.
1.5 And Gleb and Uhr were blessed by Quarkaru, for they followed the righteous path of order and peace.

Book of Temptation

2.1 But in the shadows, Evil Quarkaru, the fallen one, sought to bring chaos back into the world.
2.2 He whispered to Gleb and Uhr, tempting them with the power of discord and disruption.
2.3 And Gleb and Uhr, in their weakness, turned away from the light of Quarkaru and embraced the darkness of chaos.
2.4 But Quarkaru saw this, and his wrath was great.
2.5 He banished Evil Quarkaru to the void once more and warned Gleb and Uhr of their folly. "Turn from the path of chaos, or be lost forever," Quarkaru decreed.

Book of Redemption

3.1 And Gleb and Uhr, ashamed of their actions, sought forgiveness from Quarkaru.
3.2 Quarkaru, in his mercy, offered them a chance at redemption.
3.3 "Follow my path and spread the word of quark," he commanded, "and you shall be saved."
3.4 And so Gleb and Uhr became the first disciples, spreading the gospel of quark to the lands.
3.5 They built temples in Quarkaru's name, and the people worshipped him as the one true savior.
